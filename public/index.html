Girls Do Hack 2014

presented by the Adler Planetarium

Girls Do Hack is designed to empower young women to consider STEM careers. On November 15, 2014, teams of girls will come to the Adler Planetarium to work with women in science, technology, engineering and math careers to gain confidence in skills required for these fields.

Registration for this event will begin in October. Questions? Please email hackdays@adlerplanetarium.org
